ABOUT US


BACKGROUND

Growing up and constantly glued to the television, watching fashion shows and what seemed to me like the ceremonial red carpet, I took great interest in fashion. This spurred me into learning fashion designing which, in turn, paved way for my deep interest in the other areas of fashion. I have come to the astounding belief that fashion is a way of life. It is a culture on its own. Trendy, classic, contemporary, and whatever else you need to express, fashion encapsulates all. It is a way of making bold statements. It deserves every bit of energy put into it. I took special interest in women's fashion because not only are we expected to always look radiant, we are an exceptional set of people who should always make our marks anywhere we go.

WHAT WE DO

With years of experience in the shoe industry, our team is dedicated to curating the latest trends and classic designs, ensuring that you find the perfect pair for every occasion. Customer satisfaction is our top priority, and we strive to offer excellent service and support at all times.

At WinnieHeels, we understand that shoes are more than just a fashion statement; they are an expression of your personality and style. That's why we work tirelessly to source the best footwear from renowned brands and emerging designers, so you can always stay on top of the fashion game.
